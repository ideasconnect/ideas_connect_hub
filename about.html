About Us


"Creating a bridge between research and practice in early learning and care"

Project:

Funded by Canada’s Social Science and Humanities Research Council (SSHRC) Carried out in partnership between Conestoga College, The Region of Waterloo - Children’s Services Division and the Early Learning and Care Community Implemented over a three-year period

Goals:

To implement a collaborative model (Ideas Connect) to support the early learning and care (ELC) service sector (birth to age 12 years) navigate through the many shifts, changes and transformations To implement and evaluate an innovative partnership model of reciprocal relationships To uncover community, college and regional needs, issues, and dilemmas To optimize resources (College, Region and Community) To create an improved process for collaborative community decision making and transforming of services

Partnerships:

Professionals in the early learning and care (ELC) service sector in the Waterloo Region The Region of Waterloo Children’s Services Division Conestoga College, School of Health and Life Science and Community Services; Degree faculty and Students Bachelor of Early Learning Program Development Bachelor of Community and Criminal Justice Bachelor of Applied Health Information Science (Informatics)
