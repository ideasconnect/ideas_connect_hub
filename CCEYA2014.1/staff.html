Payroll Deductions Online Calculator

Use the Payroll Deductions Online Calculator (PDOC) to calculate federal, provincial (except for Quebec), and territorial payroll deductions. It will confirm the deductions you include on your official statement of earnings. This is an official application that the Canada Revenue Agency provides free for use.

Staff Payroll Calculator - Excel File

We provide an Excel file that includes a staff payroll calculator as well as all the other ones. This calculator will allow you to input multiple staff members to provide a better overview of payroll deductions. Note that this require Microsoft Excel or a similar spreadsheet application.
