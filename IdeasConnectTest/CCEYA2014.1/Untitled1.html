1. (1) Added: (b) a program that meets the description set out in subsection 6 (4) of the Act

29-Aug-16

6 (4) of the Act: the purpose of the program is to provide care, the program includes activities that promote recreational, artistic, musical or athletic skills  or provides religious, cultural, or linguistic instruction; the program is not operated in a person’s home; is provided for children six years old and older

(Ontario, Child Care and Early Years Act, 2014, S.O. 2014, c. 11, Sched. 1, 2015)
